How to start-up Twitter

Signing Up

  1. Fill out your name and phone number/email address. You have to use your real name because the next step will ask you to verify.
  2. Enter in the verification number that was sent to you with whatever contact information you put in. Choose a password. Make it strong and not easy to guess.
  3. Choose whether you'd like to sync your contacts. This may help you find Twitter followers.
  4. Search for interests. This will help Twitter recommend good profiles for you to follow.
  5. Twitter will provide you with some accounts you can follow based on your interests. Pick out a few you like.
  6. Simply click on the blue button on the top right to tweet, the grey bar on the top left to change your profile picture and settings, and get tweeting.

Finding Content & News

You may already be following some people if you selected some during your account set up.

You can search on the top right box for people you like. I you like Zendaya, Follow Zendaya. If you like Lil Uzi Follow Lil Uzi.
Twitter will continue to offer suggestions for who to follow as you use it. These suggestions will appear in your feed if you're using the app, or on the side of the screen if you're using the website.

If you're using Twitter on your phone, you can click on the Connect feature, which gives you suggestions based on what you've tweeted or liked. Keep adding people. There's no limit, but once you've landed between 100 and 250 accounts, you'll notice the correlation between the number of people you follow and the amount of tweets that show up in your feed

Cautions

Create Strong Passwords

  1. Create a password that's at least 10 characters long
  2. Use a mix of numerals, symbols & upper case & lower case letters
  3. Use a different password for each website & email
  4. Keep your password in a secure place

Be Wary of 3rd-Party Apps & Websites

Do you plan to use your Twitter account to register for 3rd-party websites or apps?

Be careful!

Doing so gives the apps limited access to your Twitter account. So beware!... and if you experience anything sketchy, immediately remove the app from your account.

Protect Your Phone

One of the best ways to keep all your social media accounts secure is simply locking your smartphone with a password.
That way nobody can access your accounts if your phone gets lost or stolen.
