Featured Social Media Apps



Apps Little Brief
Snapchat One of the principal features of Snapchat is that pictures and messages are usually only available for a short time before they become inaccessible to their recipients.
WhatsApp It allows users to send text messages and voice messages, make voice and video calls, and share images, documents, user locations, and other media.
Twitter Users post and interact with messages known as "tweets". Followed users can post, like, and retweet tweets, but unregistered users can only read them.

Apps Little Brief
Slack Slack offers chat rooms organized by topic, private groups, and direct messaging.Content, including files, conversations, and people, is all searchable within Slack. Users can add emojis to messages, on which other users can then click to express their reactions to messages.
Youtube It allows users to send text messages and voice messages, make voice and video calls, and share images, documents, user locations, and other media.
