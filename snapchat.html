How to start-up Snapchat

Signing Up

  1. Go to the App Store or the Google Play Store depending on your device and download the app.
  2. Open the Snapchat app and Sign up. Enter your first and last name and Sign up & Accept.
    3. Enter your birthday.
  3. Create a username that represents your name. Snapchat will suggest available usernames if the one you select is not available.
  4. Create your password.
  5. Enter your email address.
  6. Verify that you’re human: Enter your phone number. Wait for a text message with a verification code, then enter it in the app.

Making Content

After setting up your account and creating your very own bitmoji, you can creat stories and snaps.

  1. From the camera screen, tap the Stories icon.
  2. Tap the + icon on the top right.
  3. Choose to create a Group Story (for you and select friends), a Geo Story (for people within a geofenced location), an Our Story (for anyone all ober the world), or a Private Story (only available to selected friends).

A Snapchat Story is a collection of Snaps captured within the past 24 hours. By default, your Story is visible to all of your friends, and they can view the Snaps in your story as many times as they would like. You can limit who sees your Story by changing your privacy settings.

Texting

The feature allows you to connect with friends one-on-one, or have a group chat with several people. For individual chats, messages are automatically deleted once you both leave the chat or you can change it to 24 hours view and Group chat messages are deleted after 24 hours.If you don’t want a message to disappear, you can press and hold to save it.

If you want to chat with 1 friend you tap the chat button on the top right and tap your friend’s name to start a chat.

Then if you want to chat as a group you tap the chat button on the top right left. Tap the friends you’d like to include and then tap Chat.

You can aslo add your friend from the Quick Add section or type up there username.

On Snapchat we are able to make stories which are a collection of snaps again you can either make a regular story (for people that have you added to view), private story (selected friends can view), a geo story (people with your location can view) or an our story (people all over the world can view).
