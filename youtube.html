Customize Your YouTube Channel

The first step you could take after registering is to find some YouTube video channels that appeal to you and subscribe to them. That makes it easier to find and watch them later by showing links to those channels on your YouTube homepage.

What exactly is a YouTube channel? It's a collection of videos tied to a registered user on YouTube, either an individual or an organization.

The Channel guide lists popular channel categories when you first sign in. Click the Subscribe button for any channel that you want to subscribe to. Channels shown include both broad genres like pop music and specific ones like those created by individual artists.

Browse the topic categories to find more material of interest or click on your username to go to your homepage. On the left sidebar, you'll see links to popular channels, which are ones getting a lot of views, and the trending channels, too.

Watching YouTube Videos

Click on the name of any video you want to watch to go to that video's page.

It then starts playing in a small window, but you can click the full-screen button at the lower right corner to fill your entire computer screen with the videoYou can also click the middle large-screen button to enlarge the video viewing box but not make it take up your entire screen also know as theater mode.

Often, a short video commercial plays first before your chosen video shows, but you can usually click the X button or Skip to move past the commercial. Many of the commercials become skippable after five seconds of play time.
